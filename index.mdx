Tars is a customer experience platform. Build AI Agents, deploy them across web, WhatsApp, email, SMS, and voice, and let your human agents jump in when needed — all from one unified inbox.

These docs explain how to use Tars — from creating your first Agent to deploying it, monitoring conversations, and getting insights.
